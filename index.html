🎯 STUDY TIPS FROM OUR TUTORS

📊 MATHEMATICS SUCCESS

  • Practice problems daily, even just 15 minutes
  • Focus on understanding concepts, not memorizing
  • Draw diagrams and visualize problems
  • Check your work and learn from mistakes

🔬 SCIENCE MASTERY

  • Connect concepts to real-world examples
  • Use active reading strategies for textbooks
  • Practice lab calculations regularly
  • Create concept maps for complex topics

🎓 TEST PREPARATION

  • Start preparation 2-3 months early
  • Take practice tests under timed conditions
  • Focus on weak areas identified in practice
  • Review test-taking strategies regularly
